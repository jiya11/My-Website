Align

Overview

Align is an in-progress wearable device and digital platform designed to help individuals strengthen the connection between mind and body for better mental health. Our mission is to empower users with personalized insights and continuous support.

As part of UofT's Hatchery program, our team is developing a business plan grounded in real-world feedback. We are currently conducting market research and interviews with healthcare professionals and individuals with lived experience to ensure our solution addresses genuine needs. On the technical side, we're exploring the use of physiological biomarkers (like Heart Rate Variability (HRV) and Electrodermal Activity (EDA)) alongside machine learning, aiming to deliver tailored mental health guidance for each user.

Our work is ongoing, with a focus on combining cutting-edge technology, user-centered design, and scientific research to create a meaningful tool for mental well-being.
